Overview

The purpouse of this project was to implement a new, modern, more maintanable and more scalable renderer from scratch. It was to be well threaded, have a automatic streaming system and be performant. It also has to use clustered forward rendering and with a updated Disney BSDF pbr model.

The choice for going with one of the more advanced APIs had to do with the fact that I specifically wanted dynamic indexing and unbound arrays, two features which were left out of DirectX 11 and is sort of supported in OpenGL with the ARB_bindless_texture extension. And as I've had experience implementing Vulkan before I decided it was a good idea to get experience with DirectX 12 instead.
